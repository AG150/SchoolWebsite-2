School Achievements

Inter School Debate On Environment Pollution
Won first prize in Inter School Debate On Environment Pollution

The Inter School Debate on Environment Pollution is a very big Debate that happens every year with the new students that come in. If you are a new student at St. Xavier's school then you get to help debate with your opinion in the Inter School Debate on Environment Pollution. If you are not a new student and you have done this before then you get to help the new students at a certain time in the library if they need help for this debate!

Inter School Football Competition
Came second in Inter School Football Compition

The Inter School Football Compition also happens every year. You can tryout for the team once you join St. Xavier's school if you wish. You keep your position of your choice on the team for the whole time your at St. Xavier. However, other people can take your position if they are new and are choosing their position so I would recommend you to have a second position choice in mind so that you can easily change positions. Once the team is full then the team positions will not change until one person leaves the team or school.

Inter School Science Project Competition
Won second prize in Inter School Science Project Competition

The Inter School Science Project Competition is something everyone can participate in even if you are not a new student. How the Inter School Science Project Competition works is first we get at least 30 to 35 students to participate(These students are from different schools as well). There are no rounds because this competition goes until the end of the school year. The last day of school will be the finale and will determine the winner. The competition will take place every Monday and Tuesday. So I hope you participate, win, and make our school the best at science!
